Financing FAQs

Does exploring options obligate me to start a project?

No. You can review estimates first and contact us when you’re ready to discuss design and pricing.

What can be financed?

Most project costs may be eligible, including the pool, hardscape, and related improvements (varies by lender).

How do I get an accurate monthly payment?

Monthly payments depend on the approved amount, term length, and rate. Use the estimator, then we’ll confirm numbers once your scope is defined.
